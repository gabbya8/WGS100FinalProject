About Me

Hello! My name is Gabriella Alexis. I am a junior at Hunter College and this is my final project for my Intro to Gender and Sexuality Studies, WGS100 class. This project explores the history of education for Black youth in America. On each page, I describe and provide sources detailing the process of education after the Emancipation of American slaves and throughout the 20th century. Each page shows the progression in the education movement started by historical Black figures such as Frederick Douglass and goes into the Civil Rights Movement and how the fight for Black student's right of education contributed to that era.
